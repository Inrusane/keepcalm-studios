What is Bunker of Idiots?

Bunker of Idiots (also known as BOI) is a Roblox game created by KeepCalm Studios.
In this game, you are trapped in a bunker with other people, and the goal is to learn how to live in a community or participate in the general chaos! Bunker of Idiots was the first game ever made by KeepCalm Studios!
