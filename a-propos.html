About KeepCalm Studios

KeepCalm Studios is a video game development group on the Roblox platform. It is currently owned by the French founder and developer Sacha Pagnerre, more commonly known as Inrusane (or SussyChixxFr or SageOf6Sushi on Roblox).

Join us in our Roblox group!

Why this name?
The name KeepCalm comes from the fact that whenever there is a problem, we keep calm and do everything we can to fix it.
