What is Shovel Island?

Shovel Island is a game currently being developed by KeepCalm Studios. In this game, you explore a world composed of islands like Dock Island, Shovel Island, Desolation Island, and more. Defeat NPCs, collect loot, buy weapons, and level up! This adventure game has been in development for a long period of time. We hope it will be great!
